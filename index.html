The Problem

Public tenders are broken for SMEs

SMEs miss out on CHF billions in public contracts every year. Not because they can't deliver, but because finding opportunities is a full-time job.

Fragmented discovery

SIMAP, TED, cantonal portals each with different interfaces. Finding relevant tenders means checking dozens of sites daily.

Manual monitoring

No intelligent alerts. No matching. Just endless scrolling through hundreds of irrelevant postings hoping to spot the right one.

Incumbents win

Large firms have dedicated procurement teams. SMEs don't. The same contractors keep winning while new entrants never see opportunities.
